關於我們

ABOUT

OrandaCo_Electronics is a company established in 2024 with the purpose of importing advanced PDUs from Taiwan to the United States. We provide professional power distribution solutions to support the efficient operation of IT infrastructure, data centers, and industrial facilities.

INTERNATIONAL CERTIFICATION AND MARKET REPUTATIONOur supplier’s products are certified by ISO and UL, giving customers confidence in purchasing products with high stability, advanced technical capabilities, excellent quality control, and compliance with international standards.
COMPETITIVE PRICINGAs a company that directly imports products from factories in Taiwan, we are able to provide high-quality products at more competitive prices.
PROFESSIONAL SERVICEWe offer fast and efficient technical support and after-sales services, including customized product solutions.
INNOVATION AND FUTURE DEVELOPMENT POTENTIALThe company is committed to continuous innovation in collaboration with our suppliers and expanding the product line. As the demand for data centers and IT infrastructure grows, the market potential for our products is vast, especially with the global trends of digitalization and cloud technology development.
